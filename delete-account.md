# Delete your KinSense account

You can permanently delete your KinSense account and data from inside the app — no need to contact us.

1. Open **KinSense** on your iPhone.
2. Go to the **Settings** (gear icon, top-right of the Home screen).
3. Scroll to the bottom and tap **Delete Account**.
4. Confirm.

## What gets deleted

- Your account and login.
- The children you **own** and all of their logs (medications, bathroom, routines, notes).
- Your circle memberships, profile, and device/reminder tokens.

Logs you recorded on **another family's** child are kept as part of that family's history, but your name is
removed from them.

Deletion is immediate and permanent. If you can't access the app, email **info@tenhetapp.com** and we'll
delete your account for you.
