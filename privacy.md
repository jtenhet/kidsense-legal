# KidSense Privacy Policy

**Effective: June 30, 2026**

KidSense is a shared caregiving log that lets the adults caring for a child track medications, bathroom
events, routines, and notes together. This policy explains what we collect and why. We do **not** sell your
data, show ads, or track you across other apps.

## Who runs KidSense

KidSense is operated by **TenhetApp**. Contact: **info@tenhetapp.com**.

## Who uses KidSense

KidSense is for **adults** (parents, guardians, and the caregivers they invite). The account holder is an
adult. A child is a *subject* you track — children do not create accounts or use the app themselves.

## What we collect

- **Account info:** your email address (for sign-in).
- **Your name (optional):** a display name so co-caregivers see who logged what.
- **Child information you enter:** a child's first name and, optionally, a birth date and a color.
- **Care logs:** the medications, bathroom events, routines, and notes you record, including which user
  recorded each one and when.
- **Device token & timezone:** if you enable reminders, your device's push token and time zone, so
  notifications arrive at the right local time.
- **Subscription status:** whether you have KidSense Plus (via our payments processor).

We do not collect location, contacts, advertising identifiers, or analytics that identify you.

## How we use it

- To provide the shared log and sync it **live among the caregivers you invite**.
- To send the reminders you enable (and to suppress a reminder once someone logs the item).
- To manage your subscription.

## Who it's shared with

- **The caregivers you invite** to a child's circle see that child's logs. You control the circle.
- **Service providers** that operate the app on our behalf: Supabase (database, authentication, hosting),
  Apple (push notification delivery), and RevenueCat (subscription management). They process data only to
  provide these functions.

We never sell your data or share it for advertising.

## Storage & security

Data is stored with Supabase (PostgreSQL) and encrypted in transit. Access is enforced row-by-row so a
caregiver can only see children in their own circle.

## Retention & deletion

You can delete your account in **Settings → Delete Account** (see [Delete Account](./delete-account.html)).
This permanently removes your account, the children you own, and all of their logs. Logs you recorded on
**another family's** child are retained as part of that family's history, but your name is removed from them.

## Your choices

- Turn reminders on/off via your device's notification settings.
- Remove a caregiver from a child's circle at any time.
- Request access to or deletion of your data by contacting **info@tenhetapp.com**.

## Not medical advice

KidSense is a logging and reminder tool. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment, and
never recommends or calculates doses. Always follow your healthcare provider's instructions.

We'll update this page and the "Effective" date when this policy changes.
