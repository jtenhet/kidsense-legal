# KidSense Support

Need help with KidSense? We're happy to help.

**Email:** [info@tenhetapp.com](mailto:info@tenhetapp.com)

## Common questions

- **How do I invite my partner?** Settings → Caregivers → enter their email. They accept in the app after
  signing in with that email. (Sharing is part of KidSense Plus.)
- **Reminders aren't arriving.** Make sure notifications are allowed for KidSense in iOS Settings, and that
  the kid's owner has KidSense Plus. A reminder is skipped automatically if someone already logged the item.
- **The Apple Watch says to sign in on iPhone.** Open KidSense on your paired iPhone and sign in; the watch
  picks up your session automatically.
- **I logged something by mistake.** Open the activity list (Home → See all activity, or Settings →
  Activity log), then tap or swipe the entry to edit or delete it.
- **Delete my account:** see [Delete Account](./delete-account.html).

KidSense is a logging and reminder tool, not medical advice. For medical questions, contact your
healthcare provider.
